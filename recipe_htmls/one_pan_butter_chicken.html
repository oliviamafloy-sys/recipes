🍗 Chicken

One Pan Butter Chicken

↗ View original recipe
Servings
4
Time
~2 hr 30 min
Main Ingredients
Chicken, yoghurt, tomatoes, cream
Calories
~750 kcal / serve
🥬 Ingredients
Chicken Marinade
  • 800g chicken thighs, skinless & boneless
  • 1 cup full-fat Greek yoghurt
  • 2 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1–2 tsp chilli powder
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp ginger powder
  • 1 tbsp kasoori methi
  • Salt
Sauce
  • 1 large brown onion, sliced
  • 5 small tomatoes (~400g), sliced
  • 1/3 cup raw cashews (~50g)
  • 1 tbsp fresh ginger, sliced
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tbsp ghee (or butter)
  • 1/2 cup thickened cream
  • 1/2–1 cup water, as needed
To Finish
  • 1 tbsp kasoori methi
  • Fresh coriander, optional
🍳 Recipe
  1. Marinate the chicken with yoghurt, spices, kasoori methi, and salt for at least 1 hour. Overnight is even better.
  2. Heat 1 tbsp ghee or butter in a large pan. Cook the chicken for 10–15 minutes until browned and cooked through. Remove and set aside.
  3. In the same pan, cook the sliced onion for about 3 minutes until softened.
  4. Add the tomatoes, tomato paste, cashews, garlic, and ginger. Cook for around 10 minutes until everything softens and breaks down.
  5. Add a splash of water if needed, then blend the sauce until smooth.
  6. Return the sauce to the pan, cover, and simmer on low for at least 1–2 hours for the best flavour.
  7. Stir in the butter, thickened cream, extra kasoori methi, and the cooked chicken. Simmer for 5–10 minutes.
  8. Finish with chopped coriander and serve with rice or naan.

Notes

  • Tastes even better the next day.
  • Serve with rice or naan if you want a fuller meal.
  • For a lighter version, swap in light Greek yoghurt and light cream.
  • For more protein, use extra chicken or swap to chicken breast.

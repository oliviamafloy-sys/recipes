Bang-Bang Inspired Rice Noodle Salad

↗ View original recipe
Servings
2
Time
~20 min
Main Ingredients
Chicken, rice noodles, bang bang dressing
Calories
~510 kcal / serve
🥬 Ingredients
Salad
  • 100g rice noodles (pad Thai style)
  • ½ cucumber, chopped
  • 1 cup Woolworths Rainbow Shredded Coleslaw
  • 2 spring onions, thinly sliced
  • 10g crushed peanuts
  • ¼ chilli, sliced (optional)
Bang Bang Chicken
  • 250g chicken thighs, skinless & boneless
  • ¼ tbsp smoked paprika
  • ¼ tbsp garlic powder
  • ¼ tbsp onion powder
  • ¼ tbsp brown sugar
  • ¼ tbsp soy sauce
  • Salt & pepper
  • ½–1 tbsp olive oil
  • Pinch of cayenne pepper (optional)
Bang Bang Dressing
  • 2 tbsp Greek yoghurt
  • 2 tbsp sweet chilli sauce
  • ½ tbsp sriracha or Tabasco
  • ½ tbsp honey
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • Juice of ½ lime
🍳 Recipe
  1. Cook the rice noodles according to packet instructions (~8 minutes). Drain and rinse immediately with cold water to stop cooking and prevent sticking. Set aside.
  2. Coat the chicken thighs in the spices, soy sauce and olive oil. Cook over medium heat for 4–5 minutes per side until cooked through. Rest for a few minutes then slice into strips.
  3. Whisk all dressing ingredients together in a small bowl.
  4. Add the noodles, coleslaw, cucumber, spring onion and chilli to a large bowl. Top with the sliced chicken and crushed peanuts.
  5. Drizzle over the dressing just before serving. If meal prepping, keep the dressing and peanuts separate to maintain freshness — keeps for up to 4 days.

Notes

  • Great meal prep — assemble without dressing and peanuts and keep for up to 4 days.
  • Rinse noodles in cold water right away or they'll clump together.
  • Swap chicken thighs for breast to reduce calories further.
  • The dressing is also great as a dipping sauce or drizzled over grilled fish.

Honey Lemon Garlic Chicken

↗ View original recipe
Servings
2
Time
~30 min
Main Ingredients
Chicken, honey, lemon, garlic, butter
Calories
~515 kcal / serve
🥬 Ingredients
Chicken
  • 2 chicken thighs, skin-on & boneless
  • Salt & pepper
  • Cornstarch, for coating
  • Oil, for frying
Honey Lemon Garlic Sauce
  • 3 tbsp butter
  • 5 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp mirin
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • ½ tbsp lemon zest
  • ½ tsp smoked paprika
  • ½ tsp chilli flakes
  • ½ tsp black pepper
To Serve
  • Steamed rice
  • Sesame seeds
  • Chives, chopped
🍳 Recipe
  1. Pat the chicken dry with a paper towel. Season generously with salt and pepper, then coat lightly in cornstarch, shaking off any excess.
  2. Heat oil in a pan over medium-high heat. Add the chicken skin-side down and fry for 7 minutes per side until golden and crispy. Remove and set aside.
  3. In the same pan, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the garlic and sauté for 1 minute until fragrant.
  4. Add the soy sauce, mirin, honey, lemon juice, lemon zest, paprika, chilli flakes and black pepper. Simmer until the sauce thickens into a sticky glaze.
  5. Return the chicken to the pan and baste well with the sauce, spooning it over the top until fully coated.
  6. Serve over steamed rice, drizzle over extra sauce and garnish with sesame seeds and chives.

Notes

  • Pat the chicken dry before coating — this is key to crispy skin.
  • Don't rush the sauce — let it reduce properly so it sticks to the chicken.
  • Mirin can be substituted with a splash of white wine or rice wine vinegar + a pinch of sugar.
  • Make extra sauce — it's excellent drizzled over rice or steamed vegetables.
